Automated On Call Solutions

Allowing you to effortlessly and efficiently respond to up-to-date demands and changes in workforce

Basic Use

An overview of the most general use case

1.  Request comes in

You are given your own virtual phone number that allows you or whichever intended people to call in and leave a request

2.  'On Call' employee is notified

Employee will be notified by phone call and text message with a transcription and recording of the call. Employees are assigned 'On Call' dates based on whatever desired schedule.

3.  Employee responds to task

The employee will respond to the call and notify the system when they have completed the task. Metrics such as the time it takes the employee to listen to the call, time it takes to complete the task, etc, are recorded and available for viewing anytime
