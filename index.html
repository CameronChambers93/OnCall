Automated On Call Solutions

Allowing you to effortlessly and efficiently respond to requests and changes in your workforce

Basic Use

An overview of the most general use case

1.  Request call comes in

You are given your own virtual phone number that allows  whichever intended people to call in and leave a voicemail with a request

2.  'On Call' employee is notified

Employee will be notified by phone call and/or text message with a transcription and recording of the call. The 'on call' dates are assigned based on a recurring schedule

3.  Employee responds to task

The employee will respond to the call and notify the system when they have received and then completed the task. Metrics such as the time it takes the employee to listen to the call, time it takes to complete the task, etc, are recorded and available for viewing anytime

PreviousNext

Scheduler

You set up how the schedule should be generated. For instance employee A is on call for one week, then employee B for one week, then back to employee A. From then, 'on call' dates will be assigned based on that schedule. You can also change individual dates at any time on the mobile/desktop application 

Graphs

Information regarding each on call request can be queried in graph and table form. Queries can be filtered to select single employees, missed employee calls, a specific customer, etc.

Settings

Company and employee settings can be edited easily from one page. Changes made to the system are reflected immediately
